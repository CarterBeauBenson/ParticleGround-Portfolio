Carter-Beau Benson 

 
I am an ontologist, PhD candidate at the University at Buffalo, and research assistant to Dr. John Beverley and Dr.Barry Smith. I chair the National Center for Ontological Research Common Core Ontologies Working Group. I am also involved in a working group for the adoption of CCO as an IEEE standard, a working group on including ontology standards into the DoD and Intelligence community, and a working group on bridging occupation standards. My own work concerns extending Basic Formal Ontology, the Common Core Ontologies, and OBO Foundry Ontologies to the realm of Football (soccer). I am also contributing to reference ontology for games and sports.
